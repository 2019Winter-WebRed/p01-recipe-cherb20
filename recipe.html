Peanut Butter Chocalate Chip Oatmeal Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of dry oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup ground flaxseed
  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Add all the ingredients into a medium sized bowl and mix to combine well.
  2. Stick the bowl in the fridge and let it chill for about half an hour.
  3. After the chilling time, take about a tablespoon of the mixture in your hand and roll it into a ball. Repeat this until you are finished with the mixture.
  4. After you are finished store in an airtight container in the fridge.
